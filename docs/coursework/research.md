# Research (CHEM 99X)

Each semester, students must register for research credits in their research discipline:

- CHEM 990: Research: Organic.
- CHEM 991: Research: Chemical Education.
- CHEM 992: Research: Physical.
- CHEM 993: Research: Analytical.
- CHEM 994: Research: Inorganic.
- CHEM 996: Research: Biochemical.

You should register for enough research credits to reach 12 credits total if you are an M.S. student or a Ph.D. student before dissertator status. 

Dissertators should register for enough research credits to reach 3 credits total.

Courses taken on an audit basis do not count toward these totals.

Ph.D. students receive S (satisfactory progress) or U (unsatisfactory progress) grades for research credits. These grades do not count in the GPA.

M.S. students receive letter grades for research credits.
