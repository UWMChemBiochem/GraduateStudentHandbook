# Graduate Seminar (CHEM 912)

All full-time graduate students must register for CHEM 912 each semester.
CHEM 912 includes student presentations, invited colloquia, and notices of seminars from other departments and nearby institutions.

Before the degree is granted, each student must make at least two presentations:

1. One departmental seminar in CHEM 912 on a _non-research_ topic.
2. One research presentation at a regional or national meeting.

The student must seek approval for the CHEM 912 seminar topic from the major professor and _all_  divisional faculty before the semester begins. 
The student must present in CHEM 912 during the third or fourth semester to remain in good standing.

For semesters in which you are **not** presenting, register for CHEM 912 as one-credit **audit**. In the semester of the presentation, register for one credit **graded**.

!!! danger
	Registering for a graded credit when you are not presenting may result in an F grade at the end of the semester!
