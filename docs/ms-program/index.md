# Overview

The Graduate School sets the [minimum requirements for all M.S. programs]({{ URLMastersRequirements }}) and [academic policies and procedures]({{ URLAcadPolicies }}) at UWM. These requirements apply to the M.S. in Chemistry except where the department has established more stringent requirements; any such requirements are explicitly stated in this handbook.

The M.S. program consists of coursework, teaching, and completion of research resulting in the submission of a thesis ([Thesis Option](thesis-option.md)), or an approved capstone experience ([Non-thesis Option](non-thesis-option.md)).
