# Completing the M.S.

[Graduation dates and deadlines]({{ URLDeadlines }}) are published each semester by the Graduate School. If you do not meet these deadlines, your graduation may be delayed until the following semester, and you must reapply for graduation.

During the semester _before_ your planned graduation, contact {{ gradCoord }} and follow the instructions on the [Master's Graduation webpage]({{ URLMastersGraduation }}) to apply for graduation. 

## M.S. Thesis Option

The Spring and Fall semesters are the best times to schedule the thesis defense.

!!! warning
	Scheduling a defense during the summer may be difficult.

As soon as you have assembled your thesis in rough draft form, you are strongly encouraged to [submit it by the formatting deadline for review of proper formatting]({{ URLThesisFormat }}).

!!! info
	Provide a copy of the completed thesis to committee members at least one week before the defense date. Your committee will indicate whether they prefer a hard copy or an electronic version.

The thesis defense consists of a public seminar on your research, which is advertised to the department, followed by a closed session with you and your committee. The committee may ask questions on topics related to, but beyond, the thesis itself.

After the defense, make all corrections recommended by the thesis committee. 

The completed thesis must be submitted to the Graduate School by the [posted deadline]({{ URLDeadlines }}).

The student should provide {{ gradCoord }} with a digital copy of the thesis so that two printed, bound copies can be prepared for the student and the major professor.
