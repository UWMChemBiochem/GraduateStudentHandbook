# Degree Requirements

The M.S. in Chemistry requires a minimum of 30 credits beyond the bachelor’s degree. 

All degree requirements must be completed [within 5 years]({{ URLMastersTimeLimit }}) of the first enrollment in the program.

!!! note
	The department expects you to complete the requirements within 3 years.

This includes:

- [Graduate Seminar](../coursework/graduate-seminar.md).
- [Advanced Seminar](../coursework/advanced-seminar.md).
- Annual [milestones meetings](../general-departmental-policies/milestones-meetings.md).
- At least five 3-credit [graduate courses](../coursework/index.md) at the 600 level or above, including all [core courses](../coursework/core-courses.md).
- At least one semester of part-time teaching as a TA.
- Original research or capstone experience.
- [Thesis defense](thesis-option.md) (M.S. Thesis Option only).
