# Non-Thesis Option

Under the Non-thesis Option, the thesis requirement and thesis defense is replaced by an approved capstone experience, selected by the student and major professor. All other requirements are the same as those of the Thesis Option.

Examples may include:

- Write and defend a thesis-style project.
- Two survey papers based on prior professional experience in a government or industrial laboratory.
- An in-depth literature review in chemical education.

The capstone experience must be reviewed and approved by the capstone committee. The capstone committee consists of three members total, including the major professor.
