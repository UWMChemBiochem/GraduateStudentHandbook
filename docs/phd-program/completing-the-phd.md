# Completing the Ph.D.

[Graduation dates and deadlines]({{ URLDeadlines }}) are published each semester by the Graduate School. If you do not meet these deadlines, your graduation may be delayed until the following semester, and you must reapply for graduation.

During the semester _before_ your planned graduation, contact {{ gradCoord }} and follow the instructions under [Dissertation Defense]({{ URLDissDefense }}) and on the [Doctoral Graduation]({{ URLDoctGraduation }}) to schedule the defense. 

The Spring and Fall semesters are the best times to schedule the dissertation defense.

!!! warning
	Scheduling a defense during the summer may be difficult.

As soon as you have assembled the dissertation in rough draft form, you are strongly encouraged to [submit it by the formatting deadline for review of proper formatting]({{ URLThesisFormat }}).

!!! info
	Provide a copy of the completed dissertation to committee members at least two weeks before the defense date. Your committee will indicate whether they prefer a hard copy or an electronic version.

The dissertation defense consists of a public seminar on your research, which is advertised to the department, followed by a closed session with you and your committee. 

After the defense, make all corrections recommended by the dissertation committee. 

The completed dissertation must be submitted to the Graduate School by the [posted deadline]({{ URLDeadlines }}).

The student should provide {{ gradCoord }} with a digital copy of the dissertation so that two printed, bound copies can be prepared for the student and the major professor.
