# Overview

The Graduate School sets the [minimum requirements for all Ph.D. programs]({{ URLDoctRequirements }}) and [academic policies and procedures]({{ URLAcadPolicies }}) at UWM. These requirements apply to the Ph.D. in Chemistry except where the department has established more stringent requirements; any such requirements are explicitly stated in this handbook.

All Ph.D. programs consist of two main stages.
Prior to the [preliminary examination](preliminary-exam.md), students are classified by the Graduate School as [non-doctoral or doctoral]({{ URLTAClassification }}).
After passing the preliminary exam, they become [dissertators]({{ URLTAClassification }}) and focus primarily on research.
