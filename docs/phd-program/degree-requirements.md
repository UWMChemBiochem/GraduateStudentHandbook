# Degree Requirements

The Ph.D. in Chemistry requires a [minimum of 54 graduate credits beyond the bachelor’s degree]({{ URLPhDCredits }}).

All requirements for the doctoral degree must be completed [within 10 years]({{ URLPhDTimelimit }}) of the date you first enrolled in a doctoral program at UWM.

!!! note
	The department expects you to complete the requirements within 5 years.

This includes:

- Passing the [entrance examinations](../general-departmental-policies/entrance-exams.md) within the first year.
- [Graduate Seminar](../coursework/graduate-seminar.md).
- [Advanced Seminar](../coursework/advanced-seminar.md).
- Annual [milestones meetings](../general-departmental-policies/milestones-meetings.md).
- At least six 3-credit [graduate courses](../coursework/index.md) at the 600 level or above, including all [core courses](../coursework/core-courses.md).
- At least one semester of part-time teaching as a TA.
- [Preliminary exam](preliminary-exam.md).
- Original research.
- [ Pre-defense](pre-defense.md).
- [Dissertation defense](completing-the-phd.md).
