# Changing from PhD to MS

A Ph.D. student who wishes to change to the M.S. program will receive TA support only with a positive recommendation from the current major professor and explicit approval of the Graduate Subcommittee.

Per [Graduate School policy]({{ URLAcadPolicies }}), the student must formally reapply for M.S. admission and obtain approval from the Admissions Subcommittee, even if enrollment at UWM has continued without interruption.
