# Credits and Enrollment

The Graduate School requires a [minimum credit load]({{ URLCreditLoad }}) for teaching assistants and research assistants (RAs) with appointments of 33% or greater. 

Students are encouraged to register for the [Graduate School maximum of 12 credits per semester]({{ URLAcadPolicies }}) unless they have achieved dissertator status. Dissertators must enroll [each semester for 3 graduate credits]({{ URLDissertatorCredits }}). Courses taken on an audit basis do not count toward the enrollment requirements.

!!! note
	The department accepts 6 credits for M.S. students who have already met the 30-credit requirement. 

Students should consult the [Graduate School's Graduate Assistants Handbook]({{ URLGSHandbook }}) for current policies for TAs and RAs.
