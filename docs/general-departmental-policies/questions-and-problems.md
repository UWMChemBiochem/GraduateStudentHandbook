# Questions and Problems

Routine questions should be directed to the appropriate [administrative contact](../department-contacts.md):

- [Department administrator & building chair ({{ deptAdm }})](../department-contacts.md): building and facilities problems.
- [Graduate program coordinator ({{ gradCoord }})](../department-contacts.md): forms, deadlines, and graduate procedures.
- [Purchasing agent ({{ purchAgent }})](../department-contacts.md): laboratory supplies and shipping.
- [Administrative assistant ({{ admAsst }})](../department-contacts.md): payroll and TA/RA benefits.

For academic concerns, students should first speak with their major professor. If the issue involves the major professor, the student should contact the [graduate program chair ({{ gradChair }})](../department-contacts.md). Serious matters may be reviewed by the Graduate Subcommittee, the department chair, or higher university administration as needed.
