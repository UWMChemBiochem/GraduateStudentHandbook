# Orientation

All entering graduate students are expected to attend the departmental orientation program. The orientation schedule is provided in advance and may include:

- Program overview.
- Facility tours.
- Meeting with the Graduate Advising Subcommittee.
- Three [entrance exams](entrance-exams.md).
- Teaching Assistantship (TA) orientation and training.
- Faculty research presentations.

!!! note
	Students are expected to attend _all_ parts of the orientation program.
