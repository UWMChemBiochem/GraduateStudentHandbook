# First-Semester Advising

The Graduate Advising Subcommittee advises incoming students on first-semester coursework and serves as a temporary advisor until the student joins a research group.

Course recommendations may include remedial or background coursework needed to address academic deficiencies. Students must register for the recommended courses in the first semester and provide their schedules to {{ gradCoord }} for TA assignments.

!!! warning
	Students who fail to register for the recommended courses will lose their good academic standing.
