# Part-Time Students

Part-time graduate students may be granted modified requirements and timelines as needed.

- [ Entrance exams](entrance-exams.md) should be completed promptly, with flexible scheduling where appropriate.
- Students must select a [ major professor](selecting-major-professor.md) within 12 credits or four semesters (whichever comes first). The student and major professor will define a clear, feasible program of study.
- Students must maintain steady progress (typically one course per semester or equivalent research effort).
- In place of regularly attending [CHEM 912](../coursework/graduate-seminar.md), students may request permission from the graduate seminar coordinator and the Graduate Subcommittee to present two seminars, one of which may relate to their employment or thesis research.
- Thesis research may be conducted off-campus (_e.g._, at a place of employment) if it is publishable and carried out under the active supervision of the major professor.
- The thesis must clearly distinguish off-campus work and identify any contributions from technicians or collaborators; such contributions supplement but do not replace the student’s own research.
- The Ph.D. has certain [residency requirements]({{ URLDoctoralResidency }}); it cannot be completed entirely part-time.
- [Graduate School time limits]({{ URLAcadPolicies }}) still apply.
