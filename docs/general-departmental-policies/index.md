# Overview

Graduate study in the department centers on research or a capstone experience supervised by a faculty member (**major professor**). The program also includes academic and administrative procedures designed to promote fairness, consistency, and timely progress toward the degree.

This handbook summarizes departmental requirements, procedures, and resources. It is supplemented by [official UWM Graduate School policies and forms]({{ URLStudentResources }}),

Questions about the handbook or graduate procedures may be directed to the graduate program coordinator ({{ gradCoord }}), who serves as the primary contact for most general questions and forms.
