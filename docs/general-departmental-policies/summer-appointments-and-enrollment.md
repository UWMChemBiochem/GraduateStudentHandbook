# Summer Appointments and Enrollment

Summer appointments may be available depending on funding.

During any summers in which they plan to graduate, Ph.D. students [must register for 3 graduate-level credits]({{ URLDissertatorCredits }}) (dissertator rate) in the regular eight-week summer session. 

Master's students [must be registered for at least 1 graduate credit]({{ URLMastersGraduation }}) during the semester when they complete their capstone experience or defend a thesis.

Typically, these credits are research credits (CHEM 99X).

Students who do not plan to graduate during the summer session should not register for summer courses, including dissertators (even if they have received an email stating otherwise).
