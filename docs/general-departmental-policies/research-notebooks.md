# Research Notebooks

Each student must maintain a careful and complete research notebook. The notebook and related research materials are the property of the major professor and must be left with them when the student leaves the university. Students who want copies should arrange this with the major professor in advance.

!!! tip
	Consult the American Chemical Society’s (ACS) [“Writing the Laboratory Notebook” (1985) by Howard M. Kanare]({{ URLACSNotebook }}) and [“The ACS Style Guide” (2006) by Anne M. Coghill and Lorrin R. Garson (Eds.)]({{ ACSStyleGuide }}) for reference.
