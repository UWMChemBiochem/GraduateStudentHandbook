# Changing the Major Professor

In exceptional cases, a student may request a change of the major professor.

The request should be made in writing to the graduate program chair ({{ gradChair }}) and should explain the reason for the change. The current major professor, prospective major professor, and graduate program coordinator ({{ gradCoord }}) should be copied on the email. 

The Graduate Subcommittee reviews the request and forwards its recommendation to the faculty for a final decision.
