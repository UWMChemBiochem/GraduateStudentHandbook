# Good Academic Standing

Good academic standing means meeting the department’s and Graduate School’s expectations for teaching and progress toward the degree. It helps ensure students remain eligible for funding, registration, and timely graduation.

Good academic standing requires:

- A [minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0]({{ URLAcadPolicies }}).
- Satisfactory progress in research or the capstone experience as determined by the major professor.
- English language proficiency sufficient for effective teaching.
- Satisfactory teaching performance.
- Timely completion of all program milestones.

!!! warning
	Loss of good academic standing can affect funding and program status and lead to dismissal from the graduate program.
