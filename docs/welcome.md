Welcome to the graduate program in the Department of Chemistry & Biochemistry at the University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee (UWM).

This graduate student handbook summarizes departmental policies, degree expectations, and student resources for graduate students. It should be read together with the [official policies of the UWM Graduate School]({{ URLStudentResources }}), which govern general graduate education requirements.

Policies may change over time. Minor changes may take effect immediately; major changes are usually applied in a way that protects students already enrolled under earlier rules.
